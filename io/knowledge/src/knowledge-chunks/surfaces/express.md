# Creating Cards for Adobe Express

Adobe Express pricing pages showcase subscription options for the content creation platform. Cards on Express pages focus on simplicity and creative value.

## Available Variants

| Variant | Best For |
|---------|----------|
| **Simplified Pricing Express** | Clean, simple pricing presentation |
| **Full Pricing Express** | Detailed pricing with all features |

## Content Guidelines

### Audience
Express users tend to be:
- Content creators (social media, marketing)
- Small business owners
- Educators and students
- Creative beginners

### Tone and Voice
- Creative and inspiring
- Approachable and friendly
- Focus on creative possibilities, not technical specs
- Emphasize ease of use

### Simplified Pricing Express

**Purpose:** Clean, minimal pricing display

**Best practices:**
- Focus on the core value proposition
- Show price prominently
- Minimal feature list - only the essentials
- Clear, single CTA

**When to use:**
- Landing pages with limited space
- A/B tests favoring simplicity
- Mobile-first experiences

### Full Pricing Express

**Purpose:** Comprehensive pricing with all details

**Best practices:**
- Include full feature lists
- Show what's included at each tier
- Use comparison-friendly layout
- Multiple CTAs if needed

**When to use:**
- Dedicated pricing pages
- Users comparing plans in detail
- When features are the key differentiator

## Key Fields for Express Cards

### Title
- Product tier name: "Express Free", "Express Premium"
- Or value proposition: "Start Creating Today"

### Description
- Focus on creative outcomes
- "Create stunning social posts in minutes"
- "Professional designs without the learning curve"

### What's Included
- Templates and assets access
- Premium fonts and photos
- Brand kit features
- Collaboration tools

### Badge
- "Most Popular" for premium tier
- "Free" for free tier
- Campaign badges: "Limited Time Offer"

## Pricing

Express has distinct tiers:
- **Free:** No payment required
- **Premium:** Monthly/Annual subscription

Use OST to ensure correct pricing:
1. Search for Express offers
2. Select the appropriate plan
3. OST handles regional pricing automatically

### CTAs for Express
- **Get started:** Free tier signup
- **Start free trial:** Premium trial
- **Buy now:** Direct purchase
- **See all plans:** Navigate to comparison

## Publishing Workflow

1. **Create** your card in MAS Studio under the `express` surface
2. **Choose variant:** Simplified for landing pages, Full for pricing pages
3. **Set creative messaging** that resonates with creators
4. **Preview on mobile** - Express users often browse on phones
5. **Publish** to make live

## Common Issues

### Card feels too corporate
- Soften the language
- Focus on creative outcomes, not features
- Use inspiring verbs: "Create", "Design", "Share"

### Free tier not prominent enough
- Ensure "Free" badge or CTA is visible
- Don't hide the free option below paid plans

### Too much text
- Express users want quick decisions
- Cut description by 50%
- Move details to "What's Included"

## Testing

Express cards should be tested:
- On mobile devices (primary audience)
- Across different screen sizes
- With real Express pricing data
