## CME-1 Paths: Which step does a product in a region go to

1. Recommendation Step = R
2. Commitment Step = C
3. Email Step = E

- NOTE: By default the TwP Modals should link to the email step (E), unless indicated should go to recommendations step (R)
- NOTE: For merch cards on product pages (PP) (i.e. merch cards typically at the bottom of pages), compare plans, and UAR CTA should go direct to cart and link to the commitment stepC - PP in chart for ABM indicates the product should go to the commitment step in these cases (should only apply to US, UK, KR - the ROW is email step)
