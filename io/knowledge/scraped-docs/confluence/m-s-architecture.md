        # 1. Introduction


as [acom PRD](https://wiki.corp.adobe.com/display/MWPPDM/Merch+@+Scale+Phase+2) states it, goal here is to expand Merch @ Scale capabilities to XE platforms, fostering a cohesive user experience across different interfaces by utilizing current technologies. This expansion enables the activation of dynamic pricing and commerce functionalities throughout Adobe’s digital environment.


Goal is that different surfaces like adobe.com, CCD, or Adobe Home can self service not only prices or checkout URL like with m@s but full merchandising bits of content, like cards or banners.


The two main services of this platform are:


- mas/io service providing content to all our surfaces,
- m@s studio allowing easy CRUD & publication of merchandising content


after showing overall architecture, we'll go through each service's design document
