# Author Quick Start Guide

Welcome to MAS Studio! This guide will get you creating cards in minutes.

---

## Step 1: Access MAS Studio

1. Go to [MAS Studio](https://studio.mas.adobe.com) (or your organization's URL)
2. Log in with your Adobe ID
3. You'll see the Studio dashboard

**Need access?** Request access through your manager or the #merch-at-scale Slack channel.

---

## Step 2: Choose Your Surface

The first thing to decide: **where will your card appear?**

| Surface | What it is |
|---------|------------|
| **Adobe.com** | Product and pricing pages on adobe.com |
| **Adobe Home** | The Adobe Home desktop application |
| **CCD** | Creative Cloud Desktop app |
| **Express** | Adobe Express pricing pages |
| **Commerce** | Checkout and purchase flows |

Click on the surface that matches your destination.

---

## Step 3: Create Your First Card

### Option A: Start from Scratch

1. Click **"Create New Card"**
2. Select a **variant** (card type) - see [Variant Guide](variant-guide.md)
3. Fill in the fields
4. Save your work

### Option B: Copy an Existing Card

1. Find a similar card that's already working
2. Click the **"Copy"** button
3. Modify the copy for your needs
4. Save with a new name

### Option C: Use AI Creator

1. Click **"AI Creator"**
2. Describe what you need: "Create a Photoshop card for Black Friday with 40% off"
3. Review and refine the AI-generated card
4. Save when satisfied

---

## Step 4: Set Your Pricing

**Critical:** Never type prices manually. Always use the Offer Selector Tool (OST).

1. Click the **"OST"** button in the pricing section
2. Search for your product
3. Select the correct offer
4. Pricing populates automatically with correct localization

Why? OST ensures:
- Correct regional pricing
- Accurate currency conversion
- Proper promotional pricing
- Legal compliance

---

## Step 5: Preview Your Card

Before publishing, always preview:

1. Click the **"Preview"** button
2. Check how your card looks
3. Verify all content is correct
4. Test any links

Preview shows your card as it will appear to users.

---

## Step 6: Save and Publish

### Saving (Draft)
- Click **"Save"** to create a draft
- Draft cards are not visible to users
- You can continue editing later

### Publishing (Live)
- Click **"Publish"** to make the card live
- Published cards are visible on the target surface
- Allow a few minutes for cache propagation

## Common First-Timer Mistakes

### ❌ Typing prices manually
**Fix:** Always use OST for pricing

### ❌ Forgetting to publish
**Fix:** Save creates a draft; Publish makes it live

### ❌ Using the wrong variant
**Fix:** Check [Variant Guide](variant-guide.md) before starting

### ❌ Too much text
**Fix:** Keep descriptions concise; use bullet points

### ❌ Missing CTA
**Fix:** Every card needs at least one call-to-action button
