# Card Variant Guide for Authors

This guide helps you choose the right card variant for your content. Each variant is designed for a specific surface (platform) and use case.

## Quick Reference: Which Variant Should I Use?

| If you're creating content for... | Use this variant |
|-----------------------------------|------------------|
| Adobe.com: Standard product catalog cards on adobe.com | **Catalog** |
| Adobe.com: Subscription plans comparison | **Plans** |
| Adobe.com: Updated subscription plans with enhanced features | **Plans v2** |
| Adobe.com: Student-specific subscription offers | **Plans Students** |
| Adobe.com: Education institution offers | **Plans Education** |
| Adobe.com: Promotional campaigns and limited-time offers | **Special Offers** |
| Adobe Home: In-app trial and purchase prompts | **Try Buy Widget** |
| Adobe Home: Featured plans in Adobe Home | **Promoted Plans** |
| Creative Cloud Desktop: Compact upsell in Creative Cloud Desktop | **Slice** |
| Creative Cloud Desktop: Recommended products in CCD | **Suggested** |
| Creative Cloud Desktop: Minimal product cards | **Mini** |
| Commerce: Checkout flow add-ons | **Fries** |
| Express: Simple Express pricing | **Simplified Pricing Express** |
| Express: Detailed Express pricing | **Full Pricing Express** |

## Variants by Surface

### Adobe.com

*Adobe website pricing and product pages*

#### Catalog
- **When to use:** Standard product catalog cards on adobe.com
- **Description:** The most common card type for displaying products with pricing, features, and CTAs on adobe.com product pages.

#### Plans
- **When to use:** Subscription plans comparison
- **Description:** Shows subscription plans with pricing tiers, perfect for plans comparison pages.

#### Plans v2
- **When to use:** Updated subscription plans with enhanced features
- **Description:** Next generation plans card with improved layout and additional fields for richer content.

#### Plans Students
- **When to use:** Student-specific subscription offers
- **Description:** Tailored for student pricing pages with education-specific messaging and verification.

#### Plans Education
- **When to use:** Education institution offers
- **Description:** For K-12 and higher education institution pricing, with volume licensing support.

#### Special Offers
- **When to use:** Promotional campaigns and limited-time offers
- **Description:** Eye-catching promotional cards for sales events, Black Friday, seasonal campaigns.

### Adobe Home

*Adobe Home desktop app*

#### Try Buy Widget
- **When to use:** In-app trial and purchase prompts
- **Description:** Compact widget shown in Adobe Home app prompting users to try or buy products.

#### Promoted Plans
- **When to use:** Featured plans in Adobe Home
- **Description:** Highlights featured subscription plans within the Adobe Home app experience.

### Creative Cloud Desktop

*Creative Cloud Desktop app*

#### Slice
- **When to use:** Compact upsell in Creative Cloud Desktop
- **Description:** Slim horizontal card for upselling additional products within CCD.

#### Suggested
- **When to use:** Recommended products in CCD
- **Description:** Shows suggested products based on user context, displayed in Creative Cloud Desktop.

#### Mini
- **When to use:** Minimal product cards
- **Description:** Smallest card format for tight spaces, shows essential info only.

### Commerce

*Checkout and commerce flows*

#### Fries
- **When to use:** Checkout flow add-ons
- **Description:** Small add-on cards shown during checkout to upsell additional products (like "would you like fries with that?").

### Express

*Adobe Express pricing pages*

#### Simplified Pricing Express
- **When to use:** Simple Express pricing
- **Description:** Clean, simple pricing card for Adobe Express with minimal fields.

#### Full Pricing Express
- **When to use:** Detailed Express pricing
- **Description:** Complete pricing card for Adobe Express with all features and options.
